How Its Work

  • Easy Buying and Managing Orders

    Buyer orders items on website. Money is stored in smart contract.Seller receives notification of the order.

  • Order validation and Seller Confirmation

    Order is Validated,Approve and Shipped by the seller. All details are stored in Smart Contract. Buyer gets a tracking id for that order.

  • Shipping and buyer validation of received.

    All the information in Shipping Stages is monitored with smart contract. EscroPay validate the receive of goods. Seller receives money after 20 days.
